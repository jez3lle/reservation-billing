Welcome to Coastal Oasis Hotel Resort

Luxurious, affordable accommodations without compromise. Located at station 2, in the middle of Boracay Island’s world-famous white sand long beach, Henann Garden Resort is just a short walk away from the island’s attractions and provides guests sensible practicality with 273 guest rooms featuring modern aesthetics and amenities. The resort is set in expansive landscaped grounds with direct private access to the beachfront within a minute's walk.

Our Amenities

Our amenities include a swimming pool, gym, spa, and massage services. Enjoy boat tours, surfing lessons, and diving or snorkeling adventures. We also offer a conference room for business needs and access to a private beach.
