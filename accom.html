PHASE 1

PRIVATE

Book the entire property for your exclusive retreat! Phase 1 includes 2 pools, 2 houses, a pavilion, and a kubo, ensuring privacy and comfort for your group. Perfect for families, friends, or special occasions. Guests in Phase 1 can also enjoy a variety of activities available in Phase 2 Public.

PHASE 2

PUBLIC

Choose from a variety of options, including multiple rooms, cabins, and houses. Phase 2 is ideal for smaller groups or individuals looking for a cozy getaway. Enjoy a wide range of activities such as ziplining, spiderweb, bonfires and swimming, making your stay unforgettable!
