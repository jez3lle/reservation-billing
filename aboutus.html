About Us

Nestled in the heart of Brgy. Cuyambay, Tanay, Rizal, Rainbow Forest Paradise Resort and Campsite offers a refreshing escape, balancing tranquility with adventure. Founded in 2019 by Mr. Rodrigo Juco and Mrs. Josefina Tan Juco, this scenic haven invites guests to disconnect from the city and immerse themselves in nature.

Location

Rainbow Forest Paradise Resort is located in the lush countryside, nestled within a tropical forest, offering a serene retreat close to nature.
Its secluded spot provides a peaceful ambiance, yet it's easily accessible from nearby cities, making it a convenient getaway for relaxation and adventure.
